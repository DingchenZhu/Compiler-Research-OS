## Learned

- 一个好的 research OS 必须逼迫我回答：这个材料和哪个 problem、hypothesis、experiment 或 architecture decision 有关？
- Source reading 不应该只是翻译代码，而应该提取 entry point、control flow、data flow、invariant 和 design intent。
- Paper reading 不应该只写摘要，而应该输出 claim、evidence、limitation 和 reproduction plan。

English summary:

- A research OS should force every note to connect to a problem, hypothesis, experiment, or architecture decision.
- Source reading should extract entry points, control flow, data flow, invariants, and design intent.
- Paper reading should produce claims, evidence, limitations, and a reproduction plan.
